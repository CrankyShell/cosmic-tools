Our Mission

Cosmic Tools was born from a simple idea: trading tools should be powerful, accessible, and beautiful. We believe that every trader deserves access to professional-grade tools without the complexity and cost of traditional platforms.

Our mission is to create an ecosystem of trading tools that work seamlessly together, providing traders with everything they need to analyze, track, and improve their performance.

The Story

It all started with Cosmic Journal - a personal trading journal built to solve the frustration of tracking trades across multiple platforms. What began as a simple side project quickly grew into a comprehensive tool used by thousands of traders worldwide.

Recognizing the need for more integrated tools, we expanded our vision to create Cosmic Tools - a hub where traders can access all the tools they need in one place, with consistent design and seamless data sharing between tools.

The Team

Cosmic Tools is developed by a small team of passionate traders and developers who understand the challenges traders face. We combine trading experience with technical expertise to build tools that actually solve real problems.

While we're a small team, we're committed to continuous improvement and regular updates. Every feature request and bug report is taken seriously and helps shape the future of Cosmic Tools.
